Lionel MESSI

THE GOAT

Lionel Messi was born in Rosario, Argentina, in 1987. From a young age, his passion for football was clear. Despite early challenges with growth hormone deficiency, his determination set him apart.

He joined FC Barcelona at just 13, where he spent over two decades breaking records and inspiring millions. Later, he continued his success at Paris Saint-Germain and led Argentina to win the 2022 World Cup.
